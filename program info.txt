1. College of African Civilizational Studies

The College of African Civilizational Studies examines Africa’s historical, philosophical, intellectual, and cultural development from antiquity to the contemporary era. The college focuses on recovering African knowledge systems, civilizational heritage, and intellectual traditions that shaped global history.

Department of African History and Civilizations

Bachelor of African Civilizational Studies (BACS)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of African Civilizational Studies is an interdisciplinary program that explores the political, economic, cultural, and intellectual development of African societies across historical periods. Students examine ancient African civilizations, pre-colonial governance systems, colonial transformations, and contemporary African political and cultural identities.

The program equips students with analytical tools for historical research, cultural interpretation, and heritage preservation. It also encourages critical engagement with colonial historiography while advancing Pan-African perspectives on knowledge production.


---

Applicants must meet one of the following:

• Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education with at least two principal passes
• Recognized diploma in history, humanities, or cultural studies

2. College of Theology and Religious Studies

The College of Theology and Religious Studies at Sankofa Alkebulan University is dedicated to the rigorous academic study of religion, theology, and spirituality within African and global contexts. The college examines sacred texts, religious traditions, ethical systems, and the historical interaction between religion, society, and culture.

Particular emphasis is placed on African Christian theology, African traditional religions, interfaith dialogue, and the role of religion in social transformation. The college prepares students for careers in ministry, scholarship, education, community leadership, and faith-based development work.

Bachelor of Theology (BTh)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Theology provides a comprehensive academic foundation in biblical studies, historical theology, and Christian doctrine. The program combines rigorous textual analysis of sacred scriptures with historical and theological interpretation.
